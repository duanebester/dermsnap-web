Dermsnap

Dermsnap is a web application that allows users to upload images of their skin and receive a diagnosis from a dermatologist. The application is not intended to replace a visit to a dermatologist, but rather to provide a preliminary diagnosis. The application is not intended to be used for medical emergencies. If you are experiencing a medical emergency, please call 911 or visit your nearest emergency room.
